Father's Day Tribute

In wisdom's hall, you shine so bright,
A guiding star, both day and night.
In your prime, with grace you stand,
A principal, so wise, sublime.

A healer with healing hands,
You know the secrets of the lands.
From ancient herbs to modern cures,
Your knowledge and your care endures.
A father dear, a friend so true,
We celebrate and honor you.
For all the love and wisdom shared,
For every heart you've touched and cared.

Happy Father's Day, with love so deep,
In our hearts, your light we keep.
